After researching various websites, this website seemed to be good for me as it used basic HTML codes and it was easy and simple to design. Similarly, in the centre of the homepage I have kept a button to switch directly to the next page. So, these were the overall components of design that I took as a reference for my homepage. The navigation area was created with nav tag and the showcase area was made with header tag and few basic CSS components.

TECHNOLOGY : A HUGE REVOLUTION IN HUMAN LIFE

HOW HAS TECHNOLOGY AFFECTED OUR LIFE?

INTRODUCTION AND ITS PROS

Technology is defined as the practical application of good range of scientific knowledge, mainly in the field of inventions.Today, technology has optimistically affected the human life from solving complex issues to solving minor day-to-day problems, and makes it easier for completing various tasks. Technology has simplified various tasks for us and has resulted to be very beneficial because it has opened many doors of opportunites for us that were unthinkable before. Technologies are invented to ease our lives, but it equally brings challenges in actual social interaction and our health. With the initialization of technology, several things have changed radically. It has reformed the way of social interaction such as: travelling, learning, communication, business and even health. It has completelty created a drastic effect on human behaviour as well.

Technology has successfully linked all the human from all around the globe and has contributed to create globalization and has brought a huge impact on social and economic aspects of the society. Every human interaction can be carried out with the help of technology, resulting in time-management and minimizing the problems. But, technology has resulted as a boon for our society. First of all, the invention of mobile phones have made is so simple for us to connect to anyone , at anytime and from anywhere. Similarly, businesses have been profitable and effective due to the use of technology. Similarly, technology has enabled us to access several materials globally, whether they are education-based or interest-based. Overall, technology have created a great impact in the field of agriculture, transportation, communication,medical and educational sectors.For an instant exapmle, we can take this COVID situation, where the world is on hold but with the availability of technology, the economic and educational sector still progresses through online work. So, this is the power of technology.

CONS OF TECHNOLOGY

As these technological systems are designed by humans and have revolved so constantly that every youths today have built a deeply-rooted connection with technology and they are inseparable from technology. Today's youths seems to be vanished in the world of technology and seems to be unaware about the things happening in the real life. However, today's generation are spending more of their time on screen rather than off-screen.It is good to know about technology and using it for essential tasks, but when it is overused or misused it is little unacceptable. Hence, technology has caused negative impact in the human life. The access to online social media platforms has minimized actual face-to-face social interaction which has resulted in lack of social skills in today's generation. Lastly, technology creates unstable mindset causing depressing attitude and decreasing human creativity. Overall, there are pros and cons in equal proportion.

With time, I am sure that technology will be progressed more and benefit the whole globe. Lastly, technology has helped me save my time and has served for my betterment.
